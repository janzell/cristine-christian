BRIDE & GROOM
Our Story

All the best love stories have one thing in common: you have to go against the odds to get there. We first met coincidentally at CLMMRH in the year 2012, where both our fathers were admitted to the hospital ward, facing each other's beds. It was just a simple glance at each other, and here comes the year 2015, where it all started through the Facebook app. It was like destiny that we met, and we have been dating since then.

As the pages of our love story turn, we are grateful for every chapter we have written together, and we can't wait to celebrate this magical moment with you. Join us on January 5, 2024, as we celebrate our love and commitment to each other.
